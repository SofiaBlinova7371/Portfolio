This project reimagines a 1960s Philips stereo radio poster as a contemporary motion graphic, bridging retro aesthetics with modern visual storytelling. The animation was developed as part of a speculative advertising campaign responding to the nostalgia microtrend in consumer culture.

Every element of the original poster was recreated in Adobe Illustrator, then animated to preserve its visual rhythm while introducing movement.
