A spice is a story told in layers — you unfold it by noticing.

Flavor Atlas is a multisensory brand that reimagines how we explore and understand spices. More than a collection, it’s a system — uniting scent, structure, and identity into a cohesive visual and tactile language.

The branding balances clarity and warmth, with expressive typography, organic hues, and subtle motion reflecting the layered nature of flavor. Print materials anchor the brand in physicality, while the identity remains flexible, modular, and accessible across formats.

Scented cards & Aroma profiles

Each spice in the Flavor Atlas card pack is scented, acting as a sensory cue that allows users to experience flavor without tasting. The Flavor Spectrum website expands this interaction, offering an intuitive way to browse, compare, and search spice profiles.

Together, this system makes spices more approachable, memorable, and deeply understood, inviting users to explore flavor on their own terms.
